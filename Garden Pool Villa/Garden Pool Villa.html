GARDEN POOL VILLA

Private sanctuary embraced by lush tropical greenery and a serene heated pool, crafted for secluded relaxation and refined indulgence.

OVERVIEW

The Garden Pool Villa harmoniously blends indoor elegance and outdoor tranquility. Framed by manicured foliage and subtle ambient lighting, its private heated pool invites unhurried mornings and twilight swims. Inside, bespoke furnishings, natural textures, and muted tones create a calming retreat—ideal for couples seeking privacy or families desiring fluid, open space. Seamlessly integrated comforts and intuitive layout deliver effortless relaxation throughout your stay.

FACILITIES

  • Heated private outdoor pool framed by botanical garden edge
  • Expansive furnished terrace with dining + lounging zones
  • Curated minibar featuring organic & premium selections
  • Signature king bed with high-thread hypoallergenic linens
  • Adaptive mood lighting + blackout drapery system
  • Dual-vanity marble bathroom & rain spa shower
  • Freestanding deep soaking tub with garden viewpoint
  • In-room espresso atelier & artisanal tea collection
  • Integrated climate & air purification controls
  • Discrete housekeeping & turndown scheduling via panel
  • High-speed secure Wi‑Fi and multi‑device charging array
  • Premium sound system with ambient soundscapes
